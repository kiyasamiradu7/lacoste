About LACOSTE

Founded in 1933 by tennis legend René Lacoste and André Gillier, Lacoste is a symbol of effortless elegance, sport-inspired fashion, and innovation. Known globally for its iconic crocodile logo, Lacoste has become a lifestyle brand that transcends generations.

What began with a simple polo shirt has evolved into a global movement of fashion, sport, and culture. Lacoste blends classic heritage with modern designs, offering a wide range of apparel, footwear, accessories, and fragrances that reflect timeless style and authenticity.

At the heart of Lacoste is a commitment to quality, sustainability, and bold creativity. Whether on the court, in the city, or by the coast, Lacoste invites you to live beautifully and dress with confidence.
